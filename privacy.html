Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Azure AI Add-on for Google Sheets ("we," "our," or "the Add-on") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

When you use our Add-on, we collect:

  • Azure API credentials that you provide
  • Text content that you submit for analysis
  • Basic usage data to ensure service reliability

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your text analysis requests through Azure AI services
  • Maintain and improve the Add-on's functionality
  • Ensure proper rate limiting and service reliability
  • Comply with Google Workspace and Azure platform policies

Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your information:

  • Your Azure API credentials are securely stored using Google's built-in security features
  • All communications with Azure services use encrypted connections
  • We do not store analyzed text content after processing
  • Access to technical systems is strictly limited

Third-Party Services

Our Add-on integrates with:

  • Google Workspace (for spreadsheet functionality)
  • Microsoft Azure (for AI text analysis)

Please review the privacy policies of these services for additional information about how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through the Add-on or via email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our support channels.
